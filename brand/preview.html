Design Notes

why this mark works

Symbol

A diamond emblem houses an abstract F — a strong vertical spine with two clean horizontal bars. The diamond signals balance and institutional weight; the F's tall stem and rising arms carry the subtlest echo of palm geometry (trunk · fronds), honoring heritage without depicting it. A secondary diamond line adds depth without decoration. Corner ticks anchor the vertical axis like registration marks on a financial document.

Typography

FORTIS is set in a classical display serif with measured letter-spacing — timeless, confident, unafraid of scale. Wealth Management sits below in a neutral geometric sans with institutional tracking, separated by a hairline rule. The pairing is the hybrid itself: old-world serif authority married to modern-platform clarity.
